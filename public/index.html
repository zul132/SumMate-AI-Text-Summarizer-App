SumMate

Your Friendly Neighborhood Text Summarizer

Welcome to SumMate, the AI-powered tool designed to simplify and summarize your texts! This app leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence APIs to provide concise summaries of long texts. Whether you have a lengthy article, research paper, or any other text document that you want to summarize quickly, our app can assist you.

Simply paste your text into the text area below or upload your document (.txt format) and click the "Submit" button.
